STORY OF THE MAPLE SYRUP

Introduction

While there is no written confirmation of when or how was discovered. Jaques Cartier, who discovered Canada, was also the first European to write about the sugar maple and maple sap. In 1557 in one of his trips to Canada, Cartier cut a tree and tried the sweet-tasting sap. The first nations Habitants, who been eating this sap for many years before the arrive of the Europeans, told Cartier that they call it the magical tree “couton”.
